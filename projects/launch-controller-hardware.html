Launch Controller Hardware Bring-Up (SARP)

Assembled and validated mission-critical launch controller hardware for SARP, soldering SMD and through-hole components, bringing up power, and debugging Raspberry Pi connectivity for reliable ground operations.

Hardware bring-up Raspberry Pi Electronics testing

Highlights

  • Assembled and soldered two complete launch controller boards, covering SMD and through-hole parts across the controller, buck converter, and relay assemblies.
  • Troubleshot power, connectivity, and boot issues on Raspberry Pi systems with a bench supply and multimeter, verifying voltage inputs, current draw, and overall board performance.
  • Interpreted PCB designs and schematics to correctly install polarized components and maintain signal integrity.
  • Tested circuits end-to-end using a controlled power supply, validating voltage inputs and stability under expected loads.
  • Led a small team, delegating tasks and coordinating checkpoints to keep project milestones on track.
